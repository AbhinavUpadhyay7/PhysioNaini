About Us

Among the finest Physiotherapist in the city, Physiotherapy Health Care Center in Naini, Allahabad is known for offering excellent patient care. The doctor holds an experience of 15 years and has extensive knowledge in the respective field of medicine. The clinic is located centrally in Naini, a prominent locality in the city. It stands close to Near Shankar Dhaal which not only makes it convenient for people from the vicinity to consult the doctor but also for those from other neighbourhoods to seek medical guidance. There is no dearth of public modes of transport to reach the clinic from all major areas of the city.

  • The doctor's consultation fee is Rs. 200.
